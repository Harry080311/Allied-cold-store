Yes! We deliver to areas around all 5 of our branches — Nkwanta, Kpassa, Damanko, Kpandai and Tamale. WhatsApp us your location and we'll confirm if we can reach you. For large bulk orders, we offer special delivery arrangements.

There is no strict minimum order! You can order as little as 1kg or as much as you need. However, for delivery, we recommend a minimum order of GH₵ 50 to make it worth the trip. Walk-in customers can buy any quantity they like.

Ordering is super easy! You have 3 options:

  • 🛒 Online: Browse our menu, add items to your cart and click "Order via WhatsApp"
  • 📞 Call us: Dial 054 255 9031 and place your order over the phone
  • 🏪 Walk in: Visit any of our 5 branches directly

We accept the following payment methods:

  • 💵 Cash — at any of our branches
  • 📱 Mobile Money (MoMo) — MTN, Vodafone & AirtelTigo
  • 🏦 Bank Transfer — for large wholesale orders

Payment is confirmed before or upon delivery/pickup.

Our products are flash-frozen at peak freshness and can stay fresh for:

  • 🐔 Whole chicken & parts: Up to 12 months in a freezer
  • 🐟 Fish: Up to 6 months in a freezer
  • 🥩 Beef & Pork: Up to 12 months in a freezer

Always ensure your freezer is set to -18°C or below for best results.
